Join the Adventure Writers' Room - we are a group of GMs who meet in the discord voice chat, and challenge ourselves to improvise a one-shot adventure in 2 hours.

Our goal is to brainstorm fun ideas and improvise stories together in a chill, lighthearted, no-pressure environment. It’s really fun, it works, and it really helps with creating adventures! Usually, by the end of the brainstorming session, we have a solid outline of an adventure we can run for our players.

We’re looking for some friendly and creative people to join us!
